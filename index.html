Welcome to Mata Chintpurni/Bala Ji Temple

A Sanctuary of Hope and Faith

About the Temple

Nestled in the serene village of Parsapur, the Mata Chintpurni/Bala Ji Temple is more than just a place of worship; it’s a sanctuary of hope, faith, and community. This remarkable temple is dedicated primarily to Shri Mata Chintpurni Devi Ji, revered as an incarnation of Goddess Durga.

Location and Significance

Chintpurni is a small town in the Una district, approximately 40 km north of Una. It is believed that the head of Goddess Sati fell at this sacred location, making it one of the most important of the 51 Shakti Peethas in India.

Visiting the Temple

Visiting Mata Chintpurni/Bala Ji Temple is an enriching spiritual experience. This sacred space transcends identities and reminds us that the divine is inclusive.

Daily Rituals

One of the temple’s most cherished aspects is the daily pooja performed every morning and evening. Devotees are welcome to visit during these sacred rituals.
